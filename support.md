## What TaxFlow Household Does
TaxFlow Household helps you organize:
- household expenses
- receipt records
- mileage trips
- accountant-ready exports

## Common Questions

### How do I add a receipt?
Open the Receipts tab and tap the plus button. You can import an image from Photos or capture a new receipt with the camera.

### How do I review records before export?
Open the Review Queue to see items that still need attention, such as flagged expenses, unlinked receipts, or mileage trips marked for review.

### How do I export my records?
Open Reports to create:
- PDF summary exports
- CSV exports
- accountant handoff packages

### Does this app provide tax advice?
No. TaxFlow Household is an organizational tool and does not provide tax advice, accounting advice, or legal advice.

### Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored on your device. If a future version enables iCloud sync and you choose to use it, data may also be stored in your iCloud account through Apple services.
