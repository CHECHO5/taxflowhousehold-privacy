# Privacy Policy for TaxFlow Household

Effective date: March 27, 2026

TaxFlow Household helps users organize household expenses, receipts, mileage, and accountant handoff exports.

## Information We Collect
TaxFlow Household does not collect personal data on our servers.

Information you enter into the app, such as expenses, receipts, mileage, providers, dependents, notes, and exports, is stored on your device. If you enable iCloud sync in the future, your data may also be stored in your personal iCloud account through Apple services.

## Camera Access
TaxFlow Household may request access to your camera so you can capture receipt images and attach them to your records.

## Photo Access
TaxFlow Household may allow you to import receipt images from your photo library using Apple’s system photo picker.

## How Your Information Is Used
Your information is used only to provide the app’s features, including:
- storing household records
- linking receipts to expenses
- generating exports such as PDF and CSV files
- preparing accountant handoff packages

## Data Sharing
TaxFlow Household does not sell your data.
TaxFlow Household does not share your data with third-party advertisers.
TaxFlow Household does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

## Data Retention
Your records remain under your control on your device and, if enabled by you, in your iCloud account.

## Your Choices
You can edit or delete your records within the app at any time.
